Black Tusk geophysicists have expertise processing a wide range of "next generation" sensors including MetalMapper, Time-domain ElectroMagnetic Towed Array Detection System (TEMTADS2x2), and the Man-Portable Vector (MPV) sensor. We have extensive experience teaming with partner companies to carry out the following activities under our ISO accredited quality management system:

- Processing of dynamic advanced sensor data, including informed source selection
- Classification of cued advanced sensor data
- Third party quality control of advanced classification

Our processing is carried out using *UXOLab*, our in-house software for classification. *UXOLab* has been validated for use under the DoD Advanced Geophysical Classification Program (DAGCAP).
